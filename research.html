Mathematics and Computer Science

My research as an undergraduate has spanned both sides of the traditional "Theory A" / "Theory B" divide (named for the volumes of The Handbook of Theoretical Computer Science). Currently, I work with Paul Beame on communication complexity lower bounds. I have in the past worked with Eric Allender on Kolmogorov randomness and complexity and at Google on the Dart programming language, specifically Dart Web Components.
