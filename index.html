Who am I?

I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Washington. I study mathematics, computer science, and philosophy. My primary interests are in complexity, theory of computation, programming languages, and logic. I am also interested in algebra and philosophy of mathematics, among other things. Sometimes I even like to code.
I will be a member of the 2013 entering PhD class of Cornell Computer Science in the Programming Languages and Theory of Computing groups!
